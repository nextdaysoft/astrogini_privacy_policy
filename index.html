Last Updated: August 30, 2024

We collect certain personal information to facilitate one-to-one communication between you and our astrologers. This includes:

  • Personal Identification Information (PII): Such as your name, email, which are necessary to create your account and manage communication.

We use the information collected to provide, personalize, and improve our services. Specifically, we use your data for:

3. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your data. All personal and astrological data is securely stored and transmitted using encryption technologies. Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Data Retention

We do not retain your personal information and communication history, you can delete the account any time as per your choice and no data is retained at our side, if you want to delete.
